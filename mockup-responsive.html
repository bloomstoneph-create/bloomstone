Mobile improvements
44px tap targets Each day cell is 44×44px (vs ~36px before), matching Apple HIG minimum touch target guidelines. Much easier to hit on small screens.
Past dates visible and selectable Shown in muted gray but still tappable — managers often need to enter past bookings or correct errors.
Live "3 nights" counter Shown in the sticky footer as soon as both dates are selected. No mental math required.
Continuous scroll months June appears below May in the same scroll view — no tab switching or modal navigation needed.
Range highlight Blue tint fills cells 23–24 between the two selected endpoints, giving clear visual feedback of the stay duration.
Today outlined Day 19 has a blue ring outline (not filled) so it's distinguishable from a selected date at a glance.

Desktop date picker improvements
Always visible — no popup The calendar is embedded inline in the drawer's right column. Selecting dates never covers form fields.
38×34px cells (desktop) vs the current ~18–20px. Much easier to click precise dates without accidentally selecting a neighbor.
Range highlighted clearly Blue gradient fills start/end cells, and a solid blue tint fills the range between — unambiguous visual feedback.
Footer confirmation row "May 22 → May 25 · 3 nights · Click any date to change" — always visible, no guessing what was selected.
Clear button One-click reset of both dates, shown at the bottom right of the calendar, not buried in a menu.
